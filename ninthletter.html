Ninth Letter
A Literary Journal

Print Design

Ninth Letter is a literary magazine produced by a select group of students and staff of the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. Volume 10, Issue 2 is a special issue where all images are 3D (glasses are provided). I was able to help explore various typographic possibilities for text and design the table of contents (along with Denise Castañeda).

Art Directors: Brian Wiley & Maurice Meullier

Ninth Letter: Plan B

After the completion the issue above, we individually created our own 44 page alternate version of Ninth Letter which allowed us to experiment with various content from the original issue given a short deadline. My issue includes title pages for most of the original prose as well as the last lines of each story.
