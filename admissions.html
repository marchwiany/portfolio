Print & Web Design

As an intern at the University of Illinois Admissions Office, I was able to work on a wide range of projects including both print and web projects. Our goal with all these pieces was to have minimal and relevant text with a greater focus on the visuals in hopes of making students excited about coming to the University of Illinois.

Contributors: Doug Burgett (art director), Charles Hannon (senior designer), & Laura Podeschi (editorial)

Projects Shown: Viewbook, Transfer Travel, Illinois Photo Tour, & Illinois Happy Dance

Illinois Photo Tour

This website consists of 45 carefully selected images that can provide prospective and admitted students a description and look at various buildings and locations on University of Illinois campus. It has a responsive design making east to view on all device sizes.

Illinois Happy Dance

I worked on this website to promote a social media contest for admitted students to submit a video of themselves doing their happy dance. My fellow Admissions interns and I all participated in a photoshoot which served as the two images that were taken and edited by Charles Hannon.
