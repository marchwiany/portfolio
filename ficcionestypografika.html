Ficciones Typografika
Polish Posters

Print Design

My parents were both born and raised in Poland and they chose to share this culture with me and my older brother. I created this series of posters with my biligual experiece in mind. In addition, my posters were featured on Ficciones Typografika, which is a project curated by Erik Brandt to explore typography exhibited in a public setting.

Screen Prints

I also screen printed these posters on 18"x24" sheets of paper. These posters included a short description explaining three experiences that I've had because as a result of growing up in bilingual household which include having to recite Polish poems, learning two alphabets at once, and having mixed-language conversations.
