Face It
The Board Game

3D Design

How far will you go to win? Face It is a board game developed, designed, and constructed as group effort. The premise of the game is the pushes one’s comfort level by having to complete challenges that range in difficulty (easy, medium, or hard).

Contributors: Vanessa Burritt, Hyo-Ri Nam, and Heather Stickney
