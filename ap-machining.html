Advanced Precision Machining
Branding

Web Design

Advanced Precision Machining is a company that manufactures specialized products such as synchronous timing pulleys, micro-v pulleys, idler pulleys, etc. They were in need of an updated website and an official brand identity. I began by photographing their shop and samples of their products. With these visuals as the focus, I redesigned their site from scratch, and made it responsive.

Launch Date: Summer 2014
