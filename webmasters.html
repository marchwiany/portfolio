2013 Webmaster's Conference
Program & Shirt

Print Design

I designed a program and t-shirt for the 14th annual Webmasters Conference hosted at the University of Illinois. The 12 page program consisted of a schedule, keynote speakers, and descriptions of the other presentations. The t-shirt included an infographic-style back, which had various statistics regarding the conference.
